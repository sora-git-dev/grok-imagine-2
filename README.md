Upload images, audio, video, or text as creative references — all at once. Grok Imagine 2.0 understands every input together.

Tell longer stories with extended video length and seamless motion consistency across every frame.

### 🔊 Voices, Sound Effects & Ambience
Every video comes to life with synchronized audio — dialogue, sound effects, and ambient noise generated automatically.
